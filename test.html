Bio

From six to ten I can remember I wanted to be an architect, electronic engineer and superman. Now from 11 to 16, i still had those dreams but this was the time where other influnces started playing tug of war with me. I started trying to figure out who I was. Sixteen to twenty-one was a rough period, I must say. Can you remember telling your mom, I can't wait to I get big and move out. We can laugh at that now. But going through what I went through lead me to a 20 year career as a Registered Respiratoy therapist, helping others. As I continue on that journey I'm forking (do you git it) off that path as a FullStack Developer student currently at Codex Academy.
