About

Some say that or want to think that you are doomed when decisions that you make in your life leads you down the wrong path. Or that the hand that you were dealt at birth had no chance of winning at all. What if I tell you that the decisions that you made doesn't have to define your life. What if I told you that the hand you was dealt, was not a lost! Someone told me that I been through both and made it when a lot of people would've gave up. If I would have kept that same mindset, I would have agreed. No!! I'm not a victim of my environment. I changed my mindset, I wasn't dealt a bad hand, I just had to know how to play it. The decision I made, I had to own it. So the second chance that I got, that is who I am. Not the person that made those mistakes.
